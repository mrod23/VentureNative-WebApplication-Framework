About Us

Venture Native allows you to connect with city local hosts for unique, one-of-a-kind experiences.

The found team loves traveling. One of our favorite things is to go off the beaten path and do “non-touristy” things.

This way of traveling lets us truly see and experience how people really live. Finding these types of experiences can be difficult, especially if you don’t know anyone that lives in the area you're traveling to.

We decided to create Venture Native to solve this problem. Venture Native will make our way of traveling easier for everyone.
